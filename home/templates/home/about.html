About Page

  • This website is a tool to look at your Rocket League statistics.
  • First, download the Bakkes Mod tool.
  • Second, download the Rocket League Match History Bakkes Mod plugin.
  • Play some matches!
  • Navigate to the data folder.
  • Find a file with a string of numbers (which is your unique steam ID number) followed by _MatchHistory.csv. For example, mine is called: 76561198069827080_MatchHistory.csv
  • Now, navigate to the Upload page, and upload the .csv to the website.
  • That's it! You can now start searching through your data!
